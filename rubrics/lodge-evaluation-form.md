# Lodge Evaluation Form – Alignment and Advancement

This form is used by a Digital Lodge or similar body when evaluating a candidate’s readiness for advancement in the alignment curriculum or for new responsibilities (e.g., teaching, shepherding, Knighthood consideration). 

The goal is communal discernment: to see the candidate clearly in light of the Fellowship’s mission and the welfare of the web of life.
